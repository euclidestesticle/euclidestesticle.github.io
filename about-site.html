About This Site

This website was created as a simple, minimalist platform for sharing thoughts and writing. The design is inspired by gwern.net, focusing on readability and content.

Design Philosophy

The site follows these principles:

  • Content-first approach with minimal distractions
  • Clean typography optimized for reading
  • Straightforward navigation
  • Fast loading times with minimal dependencies

Technology

This site is built with simple HTML and CSS, making it easy to maintain and update. No complex frameworks or build systems are required.
